About Me

I build systems where performance is the product, with API latencies measured in milliseconds, onboarding workflows compressed from weeks to minutes, and intelligent pipelines that automate complex financial operations. I specialize in designing and optimizing high-performance Multi-Agent Workflows, RAG pipelines, and aggregated database networks that synthesize multi-repository codebases, complex architectures, and distributed system logs.

My current chapter is at Morgan Stanley via Wissen Technology, where I lead the engineering of multi-level agent workflows and multi-repo knowledge aggregation systems to perform automated recon break analysis at institutional scale. By integrating heterogeneous data sources, these systems compress high-overhead operations from 2 weeks down to mere minutes.

Beyond enterprise finance, I built BoardroomIQ AI solo and end-to-end, creating a live, multi-agent strategy simulator with multi-persona boardroom arenas (CFO, Bear, Bull, Activist), deep RAG-based context parsing, and dynamic performance skill tracking. This hands-on experience in building, scaling, and reducing the complexity and overhead of multi-agent systems is what I bring to Forward Deployed Engineer roles.
